Aims

This website aims to make SDG Indices more accsessible, which involves the information of population, Land Use Efficiency(LUE), Abstract Achieved Population Density in Expansion Areas(AAPDEA) to provide comparative analysis at different levels.

Calculation Formulas

  1. Abstract Achieved Population Density in Expansion Areas (AAPDEA) $$ AAPEDA= {Pop_{t+n}-Pop_{t} \over Urb_{t+n}-Urb_{t}} $$

  2. Land Use Efficiency (LUE) is the ratio of the land consumption rate (LCR) to the population growth rate (PGR).

    $$LCR= {ln(Urb_{t+n})-ln(Urb_{t}) \over Δt}$$

    $$PGR= {ln(Pop_{t+n})-ln(Pop_{t}) \over Δt}$$

    $$LUE= {LCR \over PGR}$$


  3. Shannon Entropy(H) is calculated based on the proportion of each land covers of specified year.

    $$p_i = {P_{type} \over P_{all}}$$

    $${H=-\sum _{i}p_{i}\log _{b}p_{i}}$$

Bookmarks

Users can save the current view as spatial bookmarks for revisiting.

  • Add bookmarks

    Input the name of your bookmark and then click "Add" to add a bookmark.

  • Delete bookmarks

    Click "" icon beside your bookmark to delete the added bookmark.
    You can also click " Clear" to delete all bookmarks.

  • Zoom to bookmarks
    Click "" icon beside your bookmark to zoom to the scale and location of the bookmark.

Layers

Differnt layers of LULC Maps, satellite images and spatial boundaries are provided in this page. Layer opacity can also be configured.

Select the layer to add to the current map. The slider is used to set the transparency.

Temporal Changes

Different periods of LULC Maps and Built-up areas are shown in this page, while users can access this information through an interactive slide bar.

  1. Load LULC map and Built-up Areas map tiles on the map.

  2. Move the slidebar to see the temporal changes of different land covers.
